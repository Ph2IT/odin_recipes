FETTUCCINE ALFREDO

DESCRIPTION

Fettuccine alfredo is one of my favorite dishes, and actually my favorite Italian dish, though I've heard they don't actually make this in Italy. What I love about this dish is the creaminess from the heavy cream and parmesan cheese mixture, and the sharp nutty flavor from the cheese.

This dish can be made a number of ways but I enjoy it with the addition of broccoli and crimini mushrooms. Many stores sell ready made alfredo sauce but I find happiness in the flavor of a homemade sauce, which is quite easy to make.

INGREDIENTS

  • heavy cream
  • oil
  • garlic, chopped
  • salt and black pepper
  • parmesan cheese, grated
  • fettuccine pasta, cooked
  • broccoli, steamed

  4. season with salt and black pepper, to taste

  7. add broccoli
